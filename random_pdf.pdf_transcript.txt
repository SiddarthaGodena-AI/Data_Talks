Renewal: This policy will automatically renew each year unless the policyholder cancels it. The policyholder may cancel the policy at any time by providing written notice to the insurance company.

Claims: To file a claim, the policyholder must submit a claim form to the insurance company. The claim form must include the following information:
The policyholder's name and policy number
The date of the illness or injury
The name of the doctor or hospital that provided treatment
The amount of the medical expenses

Benefits: The insurance company will pay the covered medical expenses up to the policy limits. The policy limits are as follows:
Lifetime maximum benefit: $1,000,000
Annual maximum benefit: $250,000
Out-of-pocket maximum: $5,000

Deductible: The policyholder is responsible for paying the first $500 of covered medical expenses each year.

Coinsurance: The policyholder is responsible for paying a coinsurance percentage of the covered medical expenses after the deductible has been met. The coinsurance percentage is 20%.
